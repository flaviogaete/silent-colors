System Requirements

The current version of Acanto is 1.0.0. and requires Live 10 (10.1+ recommended) and Max 8 (8.08+) to properly operate.

No special externals were used in the design and the device should come with all its dependencies properly embedded ("frozen"). However, it has not yet been tested on Windows –– if you run into any issues, please contact me.
